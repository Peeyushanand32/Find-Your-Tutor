Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our 24/7 support team for personalized assistance.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? expand_more
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You will continue to have access to your plan benefits until the end of your current billing period.
What is AI Compass Matching? expand_more
AI Compass uses machine learning to analyze your learning style, goals, and history to recommend the tutors most likely to help you succeed. It boasts a 95% satisfaction rate.
Do unused searches roll over? expand_more
Currently, tutor searches on the Basic plan reset at the beginning of each calendar month. Pro and Premium plans offer unlimited searches.
Are there any hidden fees? expand_more
No hidden fees. The subscription covers your access to the platform tools. Individual tutor session rates are set by the tutors themselves and are clearly displayed.
